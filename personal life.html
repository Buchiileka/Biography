Personal Life

Mary Winston was born on April 9, 1921, to Ella Winston (n√©e Scott) and Frank Winston. She grew up in Hampton, Virginia, where she graduated from high school with highest honors. Mary Jackson earned bachelor's degrees in mathematics and physical science from Hampton University in 1942. She was a member of the Alpha Kappa Alpha. Jackson served for more than 30 years as a Girl Scout leader In the 1970s she helped African American children in her community create a miniature wind tunnel for testing airplanes. Jackson was married on November 18, 1944 to Levi Jackson Sr., a sailor in the U.S. Navy, until his death in 1992. They had two children, Levi Jackson Jr. and Carolyn Marie Lewis. She died on February 11, 2005, age 83.
